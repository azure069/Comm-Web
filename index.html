About Us

Nepali Community of Belleville, Canada

The Nepali Community of Belleville is a vibrant and growing group of individuals and families who share a deep-rooted connection to Nepal while embracing life in Belleville, Ontario. Our community is built on values of unity, cultural heritage, and mutual support, creating a welcoming space for Nepalese immigrants, students, and professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a strong sense of belonging among Nepali residents in Belleville by promoting cultural exchange, supporting newcomers, and organizing community events that celebrate Nepali traditions.

What We Do

Cultural Events: We celebrate major Nepali festivals such as Dashain, Tihar, Holi, and Nepalese New Year, bringing together families and friends to share traditions, music, and food.
Community Support: We assist new immigrants by providing resources for housing, jobs, and integration into Canadian society.
Networking & Education: We offer networking opportunities for professionals, students, and entrepreneurs, helping them connect, share experiences, and grow within the community. Educational workshops, language support, and mentorship programs are also available to guide newcomers and students in their academic and professional journeys.
Charity and Volunteering: We believe in giving back to the community through various volunteer initiatives, charity events, and fundraising activities to support those in need‚Äîboth locally and in Nepal.
Sports and Recreation: Our community encourages a healthy lifestyle by organizing sports events, outdoor activities, and recreational programs that bring people together in a fun and active way.

Join Us!

Whether you are a new immigrant, a student, or a long-time resident, we welcome you to be a part of the Nepali Community of Belleville.
